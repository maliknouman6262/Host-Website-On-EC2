Frequently Asked Questions

Expand All
What is generative AI? −
Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that creates new content such as text, images, audio, and video. It learns from large datasets and generates human-like outputs based on prompts.
What is AI search? −
AI search enhances traditional search engines by using AI techniques such as natural language processing and machine learning to provide more accurate and relevant search results.
What is generative AI? −
Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that uses large datasets to generate new content such as text, images, music, and videos. Unlike traditional AI that can only recognize patterns, generative AI can create new, original data based on input prompts.
What's the difference between AI and generative AI? −
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is a broad field focused on creating machines that can perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as problem-solving or learning from experience. Generative AI, on the other hand, specifically focuses on creating new content (text, images, etc.) using algorithms and models.
What is agentic AI? −
Agentic AI refers to AI systems that act autonomously, making decisions and executing tasks without constant human intervention.
What's the future of generative AI? −
The future of generative AI includes advancements in human-like content generation, improved ethical AI, and integration across industries like healthcare, entertainment, and education.
What is machine learning? −
Machine learning is a subset of AI that enables computers to learn from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed.
What is conversational AI? −
Conversational AI enables human-like interactions between users and machines through natural language processing, allowing AI to understand and respond intelligently in conversations.
What are some benefits of AI agents? −
AI agents offer benefits such as:
  • Automating repetitive tasks
  • Enhancing customer service
  • Providing faster and more accurate decision-making
  • Improving operational efficiency
What’s the difference between an AI agent and a chatbot? −
While both AI agents and chatbots interact with users, AI agents are more advanced. They can perform complex tasks, learn over time, and integrate with enterprise systems, whereas chatbots mainly handle predefined conversational flows.
What is an AI agent builder? −
An AI agent builder is a tool or platform that allows developers to create and customize AI agents by training them with data, defining workflows, and integrating them with business applications.
How are AI agents built? −
AI agents are built using machine learning models, rule-based systems, and APIs. The process involves training the AI on relevant data, defining rules, and deploying it for real-time decision-making.
How do AI agents work? −
AI agents process data, analyze inputs, and take actions based on predefined rules or learned patterns. They can interact with users, automate processes, and continuously improve through machine learning.
