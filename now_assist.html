What is generative AI? −
Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that uses large datasets to generate new content such as text, images, music, and videos. Unlike traditional AI that can only recognize patterns, generative AI can create new, original data based on input prompts.
What's the difference between AI and generative AI? −
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is a broad field focused on creating machines that can perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as problem-solving or learning from experience. Generative AI, on the other hand, specifically focuses on creating new content (text, images, etc.) using algorithms and models.
Does Now Assist support FedRamp and GCC customers? −
Yes, Now Assist is fully compliant with FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program) and GCC (Government Community Cloud) standards, ensuring that it meets the necessary security requirements for federal and government customers.
What languages are supported by Now Assist? −
Now Assist supports multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and several others. The platform is designed to be scalable to integrate with multiple regional languages for global customer support.
What AI models does ServiceNow use and support? −
ServiceNow uses various AI models including natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, and deep learning models. These models are employed to enhance user experiences, streamline workflows, and improve decision-making across various business processes.
What is machine learning? −
Machine learning is a subset of AI that focuses on algorithms and statistical models that allow computers to improve their performance on a specific task over time through data-driven insights. It enables systems to learn from past data and make predictions without explicit programming.
What is an LLM? −
An LLM (Large Language Model) is a type of deep learning model that processes and generates human language text. LLMs like GPT-3 are trained on vast amounts of text data and are capable of performing a wide range of natural language processing tasks such as text generation, translation, and summarization.
What is an AI chatbot? −
An AI chatbot is a program that uses artificial intelligence to simulate conversation with users. It can answer questions, provide customer support, and perform tasks using natural language processing to understand user input and generate responses.
What are AI agents? −
AI agents are intelligent programs that can perform specific tasks, automate processes, and interact with users. They can analyze data, make decisions, and adapt over time to improve their performance.
What's the difference between a Now Assist-powered AI chatbot and ServiceNow AI Agents? −
A Now Assist-powered AI chatbot is designed to handle simple, rule-based tasks such as answering FAQs or providing support in a conversational format. ServiceNow AI Agents, on the other hand, are more advanced and can perform complex workflows, automate business processes, and integrate with enterprise systems for real-time decision-making and operational efficiency.
